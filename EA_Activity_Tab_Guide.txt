EA ACTIVITY TAB — USER GUIDE
=============================


WHAT IT DOES
------------
The EA Activity tab reads your Obsidian meeting notes from the past quarter
and generates a pre-formatted activity table you can copy into Salesforce
for EA Engagement reporting. It automatically identifies reportable
activities, picks the correct Type and Subtype from the SFDC taxonomy,
and writes a comment for each row.


STEP-BY-STEP
------------
1. Click "EA Activity" in the top navigation bar.

2. Select the account folder you want to report on using the folder
   picker (e.g. the folder for L3Harris, NGC, Frontgrade, etc.).

3. Click "Scan Folder."
   The app finds all notes dated within the past 3 months.

4. Review the scrub panel (if it appears).
   Lines containing keywords tied to other accounts are flagged for
   removal. Check any flagged line you want to keep before generating.

5. Choose a model.
   - Haiku  — faster, lower cost
   - Sonnet — better quality, recommended for this report

6. Click "Generate EA Activity Report."
   Rows stream in live as Claude reads through your notes.

7. When finished, click "Copy" to copy the Markdown table, or
   "Save to Obsidian" to write it as a file in your vault.


WHAT YOU GET
------------
A table with one row per reportable activity:

  | Account | Event Date | Engagement Title | Type | Subtype | Comments |

- Account          the account name from the folder you selected
- Event Date       date of the meeting, pulled from the note heading
- Engagement Title short name for the activity (SFDC-style)
- Type             one of the 6 SFDC EA Engagement Types
- Subtype          specific subtype within that Type
- Comments         up to 1,000 characters, written for an executive reader


WHAT GETS INCLUDED
------------------
- Customer meetings, including EA Admin syncs and governance calls
- User group sessions and demo days
- Internal NI sessions that produced a clear decision or outcome
- Training planning sessions and campaign activities
- Escalations and risk management activities
- Case study and ROI capture sessions

WHAT GETS LEFT OUT
------------------
- Routine emails or check-ins with no outcome
- Duplicate rows if the same session appears in multiple notes
- Notes from other accounts (filtered out automatically)
- Low-value logistics with no reportable substance


ABOUT EA ADMINS
---------------
EA Admins are IT administrators on the customer side (e.g. at L3Harris,
Northrop Grumman, Lockheed Martin, Frontgrade) who manage NI licensing
and EA logistics for their company. They are not NI employees.
Meetings with EA Admins are always classified as customer-facing —
either "EA Admin Sync" or "EA Admin Onboarding" depending on context.


SAVING
------
Files are saved to your vault inside the selected account folder as:
  EA Activity Report YYYY-MM-DD.md
